About Autotech

Autotech was founded in 2021. Since cars became common people have struggled to find good mechanics at fair prices. Every one at some point has felt ripped off by a mechanic. Autotech rids all these issues people have had previously and makes it easy for people to book there cars in for what ever work it needs. Autotech only advertises approved mechanics and allows you to select what suits you the best ensuring good quality and fair pricing.
